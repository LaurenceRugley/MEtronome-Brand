Working Musician · Since 2012

Rebel Shakedown

Roots · Rock · Reggae · Huntington Beach, CA

Rebel ShakeDown formed on August 20, 2014 in Huntington Beach, CA. I was recruited as the band's dedicated keyboards player to complete a lineup of musicians who'd been sharing stages together since 2009 across various well-known SoCal reggae acts — collectively bringing 30 years of tour experience to the table.

We've shared the stage with national acts including HIRIE, Fortunate Youth, Eli-Mac, Leilani Wolfgram, and Rome of Sublime w/ Rome. Our debut EP World On Fire was produced by Lewis Richards at 17th Street Recording Studio and featured members of Seedless and Fortunate Youth percussionist Travi Bongo. Over 10 years we've built a catalog of 40+ original compositions with collaborations featuring Shwayze, Kash'd Out, Dubbest, Karim Israel of Arise Roots, Dread Kennedy, and more. The rhythmic and acoustic intuition I've built on stage and in the studio directly shapes every timing and audio decision in MEtronome.
