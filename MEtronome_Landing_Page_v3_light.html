Formed August 20, 2014  ·  30 years combined tour experience

Rebel ShakeDown formed in 2014 in Huntington Beach, CA. I was recruited as the band's dedicated keyboards player to complete a lineup whose members bring 30 years of combined tour experience from prior well-known SoCal reggae acts.

Over 10 years we've built a catalog of 40+ original compositions with collaborations featuring Shwayze, Kash'd Out, Dubbest, Seedless, Karim Israel of Arise Roots, and Dread Kennedy. We've shared the stage with HIRIE, Fortunate Youth, Eli-Mac, Leilani Wolfgram, and Rome of Sublime w/ Rome. Debut EP World On Fire produced by Lewis Richards at 17th Street Recording Studio.
